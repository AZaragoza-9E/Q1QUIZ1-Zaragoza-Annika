Inside Out 2

What is Inside Out 2 about?


Inside Out 2 is basically a story of Riley who is now a 13 year old and is going through being a teenager and being in Highschool.
However when puberty recently got into Riley, new emotions came in the Headquarters such as Anxiety, Envy, Embarrassment, and Ennui.
And ever since, as Riley tried to bottle up her old emotions like Joy, Sadness, Disgust, Anger, and Fear.
And ever since the new emotions took control, this has caused Riley to have strange shifts in her behavior.
She becomes an entirely different person from being a sunny and bright teen
to an anxious and obsessed teenager obsessed with fitting in the cool crowd, even if it means leaving her old friends.
